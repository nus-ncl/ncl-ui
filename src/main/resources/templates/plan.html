Features

1-click Experiment Setups

Spend less time on tedious experiment setups and more for conducting your experiments. From replicating an experiment conducted by another researcher before to reusing a particular network topology, it all can be done with just one click of the mouse.

Infrastructure with 200+ nodes,GPU's and High end servers

NCL facilitates faster research by providing necessary tools and resources to conduct the experiments in the cloud. We provide compute clusters of 200+ nodes,GPU's and High end servers on demand without large capital investments.

 

 

 

Data and Data Repository

Browse,search and contribute to our collection of datasets contributed by other researchers like you. With data ranging from internet traffic data to mobile app data,find and use the one most suitable for you and validate your research.

Security

As a cybersecurity lab, security is of our utmost concern. All experiment session are encrypted and carried out in a contained environment, separated from other experiments, as well as the internet.

Tools & Services

50+ Ready-to-use Common Vulnerabilities and Exposure CVE Environments

What is this?

  • A description of known vulnerabilities that can be found on the internet.
  • Ready-to-use CVEs are deployable environments where the vulnerability exists and are difficult to build.

Software-defined Networking (SDN) Provisioning Tools

What is this ?

  • The SDN technology relies on the hardware features on switches. Such SDN switches on large networks are not easily available.
  • NCL has 14 SDN switches connecting 200 nodes.

Advance Red Team Environment

What is this?

  • An environment for various red team activities like Lateral Movement, Obfuscation, Evasion and latest vulnerabilities like Zero Logon
  • Comes with an Active Directory Environment

Virtual Mid-size Enterprise Network

What is this ?

  • A medium-sized virtual network that can simulate multiple real life scenarios.
  • Useful for training and validation.

Ready-to-use Penetration Testing Environment & Tools

What is this?

  • Pentest is an authorized simulated attack on a computer system, performed to evaluate the security of the system.
  • 24*7,online web based portal to access virtual network environment with tools and vulnerabilities pre-installed and configured to learn and practice Pentest techniques.
